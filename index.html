Our Innovation Journey

Since 2003, our company has been supplying agricultural and horticultural products such as fertilizers, pesticides, seeds, agricultural machinery, drip irrigation systems, and other netting or greenhouse equipment. Since 2008, after receiving technology transfer from Australia, Botanium Agro-Tech has begun producing organic fertilizers and high-concentration foliar fertilizers. In addition to providing pest and disease management solutions for organic agriculture, the company also offers customized formulations for large plantations, enabling crops like oil palm and rice to fully realize their potential and achieve maximum yields.
