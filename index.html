Smart Agriculture for a Sustainable Future

Since 2003, Botanium Agro-Tech has empowered Malaysian agriculture through innovation, technology, and trusted expertise.

Welcome to Botanium Agro-Tech

We supply fertilizers, pesticides, seeds, machinery, irrigation systems, and greenhouse equipment to clients across Malaysia. Our clientele ranges from small traditional farmers to modern commercial farms adopting precision agriculture.

Our Innovation Journey

In 2008, Botanium adopted Australian technology and began producing organic and concentrated foliar fertilizers. We offer unique formulations for oil palm and paddy, maximizing yield and sustainability for commercial growers.
